ChinUp Club

Connect with others who understand life with a chronic illness.

Want to be a part of our safe and supportive community? Give us your name and email address to be notified as soon the club opens.

ChinUp Club is a place to make friends with others who suffer from chronic illnesses. You and your new circle will come to lean on one another for support and understanding. You'll email, send cards via old-fashioned mail, and even meet up in person.

You'll become a force for good in the lives of others who are dealing with similar struggles, and they'll become a support system to help you through your hard times.

You're not alone.

Sometimes it may feel that way, but many people experience similar challenges and frustrations.

It's why we're building ChinUp Club: to help you find others like you to share in your triumphs and support you in bad times. Make friends and always keep your chin up.
